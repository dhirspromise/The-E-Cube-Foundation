Dhir's
Promise

Organic vegetables, heritage herbs, and a living Himalayan seed collection — grown with intention in the soils of Uttarakhand.

Our Farm

A Promise to the Land

Dhir's Promise is an organic farm nestled in Uttarakhand, cultivating fresh vegetables, medicinal herbs, and a rare Himalayan seed collection using entirely natural, chemical-free methods.

Every crop is grown with a deep respect for the mountain ecosystem — honouring indigenous seeds, traditional farming wisdom, and the soil that has sustained communities here for generations.

Through direct partnerships with local restaurants and community outreach, Dhir's Promise brings organic Himalayan produce to tables that value provenance, purity, and story.

What We Grow
From the Mountain to the Table
Seasonal organic vegetables · Fresh culinary and medicinal herbs · A curated Himalayan seed collection preserving regional plant heritage.
How We Sell
Direct & Intentional
We partner directly with local restaurants and reach customers through WhatsApp and our website — keeping every transaction personal, transparent, and traceable.

The Himalayan
Village School

An independent learning and resource centre where workshops, outdoor discovery, and hands-on science bring education to life.

Our Approach

It Takes a Village to Raise a Child

The Himalayan Village School (HVS) operates on a simple but powerful belief: children learn best when education is experiential, joyful, and rooted in the world around them.

Rather than a traditional classroom model, HVS is an independent resource centre that runs immersive workshops, outdoor learning sessions, science activities, and collaborative programmes across schools in the region.

From rocketry to outdoor orienteering, HVS is where curiosity becomes achievement.

Highlight Achievement
The Himalayan Rocketeers
A team of young students from the HVS community competed in the NASA Space Apps Challenge — building and racing a rover to clinch first place.

Mountains

Orienteering, adventure networks, and guided treks — taking young people and curious minds into the wild heart of the Himalayas.

PYAN

Positive Young Adventurers' Network

PYAN is a programme built around a simple idea: young people grow when they're challenged by real terrain, real navigation, and real teamwork.

So far, PYAN has conducted an orienteering session on the mountain — where students learned the fundamentals of map-reading, compass work, and wayfinding in actual Himalayan terrain. This is just the beginning.

The vision for PYAN is a growing network of young adventurers across Uttarakhand, building outdoor competence and confidence together.

First Session
Mountain Orienteering
Students took to real Himalayan terrain to learn basic orienteering skills — map reading, compass use, and terrain navigation. A hands-on introduction to reading the mountain.
Guided Treks

Walk the Himalayan Villages

The E Cube Foundation also offers guided treks through Himalayan villages and high-altitude terrain — led by people who know this landscape intimately.

These aren't tourist treks. They're journeys through working landscapes — past organic farms, ancient trails, and communities that have lived in the mountains for centuries.

Whether for individuals, groups, or school programmes, our guided treks are an immersive encounter with the real Himalayas.

Village Routes
Himalayan Village Treks
Multi-stop routes through traditional villages, terraced farmland, and forested ridgelines — guided by local experts.
Terrain Treks
High-Altitude Terrain
For those seeking a more challenging encounter with the mountains — guided high-terrain routes suited to varying fitness levels.
